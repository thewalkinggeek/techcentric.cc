Biography

Bridging People and Technology Together

Jonathan Schoenberger is a technology specialist with 30 years of hands-on IT experience. He graduated with honors from Westwood College where he received an Associate Degree of Applied Science in Network Engineering.

Mr. Schoenberger spent over 20 years as an IT Consultant and Technician, servicing a wide range of technology needs to companies and clients. With his diverse knowledge base he has helped clients increase productivity, efficiency, and profitability through technology and service application.

He is currently a Technical Client Support Specialist with The Storage Group, a digital marketing and SaaS company. There he provides technical and development support to clients and internal teams. In his personal time he is exploring the use of Large Language Models in development, project management, and problem solving.
